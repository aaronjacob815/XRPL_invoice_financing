# XRPL_invoice_financing

Project for the Ripple track of the Finnovator hackathon @ Imperial, June '26.

A decentralised invoice-financing marketplace built on the XRP Ledger, linking freelancers, companies, and lenders together.

**Abstracted description**: Someone has an invoice for later but needs money now, The company that owes the money mints a token representing that invoice; the freelancer sells it to a competing marketplace of lenders for cash today; the lender collects the full amount from the company at the due date.

Still under development!

Written by Aaron Jacob, Sebastian Kember, Astha Nihar, and Naveen Goswami.
